David James

Managing Director & Founder

David James founded DJ Advisory Services in 2018 after nearly two decades in middle-market M&A. His career spans transaction advisory at a Big Four firm, director-level M&A execution at a boutique investment bank, and now, running his own practice.

He has advised on 40+ transactions totaling more than $2 billion in enterprise value, across industries ranging from industrial services to healthcare technology. His clients include founder-owners, private equity firms, and strategic acquirers.

David holds an MBA in Finance and Strategy and is a Certified Merger & Acquisition Advisor (CM&AA). He is based in New York and serves clients nationwide.
