Sell-Side M&A Advisory

When you're ready to sell, we make sure you're ready.

Our sell-side process is designed to maximize value while minimizing disruption to your business. We've refined this process over 40+ transactions, and it works.

Preparation & Positioning

  • Financial recasting to present normalized earnings
  • Operational optimization recommendations
  • Narrative development — crafting your company's story

Buyer Identification

  • Strategic and financial buyer mapping
  • Ranking by fit, capability, and likelihood to transact
  • Confidential outreach and NDAs

Marketing & Outreach

  • Confidential Information Memorandum (CIM)
  • Teaser campaigns for initial interest
  • Targeted introductions to qualified buyers

Management Presentations

  • Preparation of management team for buyer meetings
  • Anticipating tough questions and preparing responses
  • Facilitating productive, structured conversations

Negotiation & Closing

  • LOI through definitive agreement
  • Your interests protected at every term
  • Coordination with legal and tax advisors

Typical engagement size: $10M–$250M enterprise value

Buy-Side Advisory

The right acquisition can transform a business. The wrong one can destroy it.

We help buyers identify, evaluate, and execute acquisitions with confidence. Our process is designed to find the right target at the right price, with the right structure.

Search & Screening

  • Define acquisition criteria with your strategic goals
  • Source targets through proprietary databases and relationships
  • Filter for operational, financial, and cultural fit

Initial Diligence

  • Financial, operational, and cultural assessment
  • Pre-LOI analysis to avoid wasted time
  • Red-flag identification and risk quantification

Valuation & Offer Strategy

  • Data-backed pricing using comparable transactions
  • Negotiation positioning and walk-away analysis
  • Structure optimization — cash, stock, earnouts, etc.

Full Diligence Management

  • Coordinate legal, tax, and operational diligence
  • Manage information flow and Q&A with target
  • Identify and quantify risks before they become surprises

Closing Support

  • SPA negotiation and key term advocacy
  • Transition planning
  • Integration advisory for the first 90 days

Valuation & Fairness Opinions

What's it worth? The answer depends on who's asking — and why.

We provide independent valuation analysis grounded in rigorous financial modeling, comparable transaction analysis, and deep industry knowledge. Our valuations are defensible — because we can defend them.

Transaction Pricing

Support for buyers and sellers in negotiation. We model multiple scenarios, stress-test assumptions, and provide a clear range of value that reflects the specific deal dynamics at play.

Fairness Opinions

Board-level opinions for shareholders and fiduciaries. Our fairness opinions are prepared with the rigor and independence expected by boards, special committees, and regulators.

Estate & Tax Planning

Defensible valuations for IRS and regulatory compliance. We work with your tax and legal advisors to ensure valuations withstand scrutiny.

Litigation Support

Expert witness and damages analysis. When valuation disputes end up in court, our analysis stands up to cross-examination.

Capital Raising Advisory

The right capital structure can accelerate growth. The wrong one can handcuff it.
