The Firm

DJ Advisory Services is a boutique M&A advisory firm based in New York, serving business owners and investors nationwide. We specialize in middle-market transactions where the stakes are high and the margin for error is thin.

Unlike larger firms that pass clients between junior bankers, DJ Advisory Services provides partner-level attention from the first meeting through the wire transfer. We take on a limited number of engagements each year — typically four to six — to ensure every client receives the focus and expertise they deserve.

Our Approach

Every transaction is different, but our process is built on principles that never change:

Deep Diligence, Early. We understand your business before we talk to a single buyer. We know your strengths, your risks, and your story — because a well-prepared seller commands a premium.

Tailored Process Design. No two transactions are the same. We design the process around your goals, timeline, and risk tolerance — whether that's a competitive auction or a quiet, bilateral negotiation.

Transparent Communication. You see our analysis, our thinking, and our concerns. No black boxes. Every recommendation comes with the data and reasoning behind it.

Relentless Execution. We manage the details so you can focus on running your business. From LOI through definitive agreement, we ensure nothing falls through the cracks.

Why DJ Advisory Services?

Experience. 20+ years in middle-market M&A across sell-side, buy-side, and capital raising. We've seen what works, what doesn't, and what separates a good deal from a great one.

Focus. We don't spread ourselves thin. Every engagement gets senior attention from day one to close.

Results. 40+ closed transactions, $2B+ in total value advised. We measure success by closed deals and satisfied clients, not pitch decks sent.

Discretion. Your business is your business. We protect your confidentiality at every step — from initial outreach through post-close integration.
